About

Hi, my name is Courtney Carter (She/hers). I am Grinnell College senior graduating in May 2021 with a Bachelors of Arts degree in Physics and Studio Art. My primary research interests are in Galactic archaeology and galaxy evolution. In particular, I am interested in using large-scale survey data and cosmological simulations to uncover the assembly history of the Milk Way by studying the chemistry and dynamics of ancient stellar populations.
